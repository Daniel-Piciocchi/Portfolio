Who Am I?

I am a full stack developer, in training. The meaning of those three words are at present a mystery to me, yet by the end of this course I hope to understand them by at least 10%, equating to how much I understood of my philosophy degree.

On any given day, I wake up early and attempt to meditate, followed by a brisk walk to my local cafe for a mocha, where after several years I am still asked my name.

I also enjoy buying books ill never read, calisthenics, house plants and the constant battle to prevent their demise, journaling, pretending I am not subservient to the demands of my cats, learning Spanish, playing melancholy songs on guitar, and competitive gaming.

If you feel like chatting about existential dread, hola at me.
